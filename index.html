Getting Started with React.js

The easiest way to start developing is by using the expo online editor.

Step 1: Go to snack.expo.io

Step 2: On the top right corner, click on the black square and then click on login

Step 3: Then click on 'Sign up for Expo'

Step 4: Enter all your login details

Now that you have logged in, you can start creating your own snacks
